Rain

One afternoon I said to myself,
"Why isn't the rain raining heavier?"
Does the cloud not seem to cry today?
or does it not?

One afternoon I said to myself,
"Why isn't my life more sunny?"
Is it because the cloud covers my cloudy heart?
or does it not?

I saw myself in a dark plane.
As I see myself dancing again.
Now this feeling is a thing.
To get me wondering on how to create this poem again.
